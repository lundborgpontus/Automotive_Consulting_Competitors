Each consulting firm is scored 0–100 across eight weighted dimensions, specifically calibrated for the Swedish automotive consulting market. Scores are based on publicly available evidence, analyst reports, and systematic inference logic.

Inference Logic: Where direct Swedish automotive revenue data is unavailable, scores are inferred from: (a) Nordic office headcount proxies, (b) publicly confirmed client relationships with Volvo Cars/Group, Scania, Polestar, Autoliv, SKF, (c) SAP/cloud/Microsoft/AWS partner tier status in Nordics, (d) published case studies and press releases, (e) analyst rankings from IDC MarketScape, HFS Horizons, Everest Group, and Gartner.

Key Market Intelligence Findings
🏆 Strategy Dominance

McKinsey leads in pure automotive strategy for Swedish OEMs, leveraging deep Volvo Cars capability-building engagements and global automotive practice. BCG and Roland Berger are strong #2/#3 with specific EV and SDV strategy credentials in Sweden.

⚡ Technology & SDV

Capgemini is the undisputed leader in technology delivery for Swedish automotive — IDC MarketScape Leader in SDV engineering, selected by Volvo Cars for digital/cloud transformation, and Aurobay SAP S/4HANA implementation. Accenture is #2 with Zenuity and cloud scale.

🌱 Sustainability & EV

Roland Berger's 2024 EV Charging Index for Sweden and deep decarbonization advisory puts them ahead in pure EV strategy. Deloitte and KPMG lead on sustainability reporting and net-zero transformation at board level for Swedish industrials.

💼 Deal Advisory

EY's fairness opinion for Volvo Cars (2025 EGM) and KPMG's Polestar audit relationship establish them as the dominant M&A advisory players in Swedish automotive. PwC's Volvo Cars care experience and Bildahl transaction demonstrate strong deal credentials.

🏭 Manufacturing & SC

Accenture and Capgemini dominate manufacturing digitalization delivery. McKinsey and BCG lead supply chain strategy. EY is ranked #1 by HFS Horizons 2025 in Intelligent Supply Chain Services globally, translating to strong Swedish industrial credentials.

🔬 Engineering Niche

Arthur D. Little leads in deep R&D and engineering advisory for Swedish industrials — their Volvo Group CIO thought leadership and specialized automotive engineering methodology is unmatched among pure advisory firms in the Nordics.
